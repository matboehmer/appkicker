Science anybody?

Thanks for choosing AppKicker! This is a twofold app: On the one hand it provides you with shortcuts to recent apps. On the other hand it is a research app that helps us to investigate mobile app usage. By using this app and accepting the following disclaimer you agree on taking part in an academic research study as long as you have the Recent Apps Widget installed.

In this study and by means of this app we collect data on your application usage. We use it to inform the algorithms of the widget, to improve the app itself and to gain insights into mobile application usage. In detail, we are collecting the following pieces of anonymized information on a central server: a unique identifier, the Android version of your device, the model of your device, the screen size of your device, installations, updates, removals of applications, the particular usage of apps, the location of such events, an the state of your GPS, bluetooth, headphones and WiFi hardware. Additionally, we keep track of every additional piece of information that you explicitly enter within the application itself, e.g. your age and your sex.

At any point in time you can withdraw from submitting your data. For that case, please remove the app from your device. At any point in time you have access to the data that we have recorded. For that case, please contact us via email (mail@appkicker.org). By clicking on the accept button you also agree, that we may use the submitted data for anonymous statistical analysis and for the scientific publication of our findings.

This app is part of a research project of the German Research Center for Artificial Intelligence.

For more information, please visit our web page appkicker.org.
